# AI Writing Patterns — Catálogo de Referência

Fonte: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Signs_of_AI_writing (WikiProject AI Cleanup)

Insight central: "LLMs usam algoritmos estatísticos para adivinhar o que vem a seguir. O resultado tende para o resultado mais estatisticamente provável que se aplica ao maior número de casos."

---

## Padrões de conteúdo

### 1. Ênfase indevida em significado e legado

Palavras de alerta: stands/serves as, is a testament/reminder, vital/significant/crucial/pivotal/key role/moment, underscores/highlights its importance, reflects broader, symbolizing its ongoing/enduring/lasting, setting the stage for, key turning point, evolving landscape, indelible mark, deeply rooted

Problema: infla a importância de aspectos arbitrários atribuindo-lhes simbolismo mais amplo.

Antes: "The Statistical Institute was established in 1989, marking a pivotal moment in the evolution of regional statistics, part of a broader movement to decentralize administrative functions."
Depois: "The Statistical Institute was established in 1989 to publish regional statistics independently."

---

### 2. Ênfase indevida em notoriedade e cobertura de mídia

Palavras de alerta: independent coverage, local/regional/national media outlets, active social media presence

Problema: afirma notoriedade sem contexto real.

Antes: "Her views have been cited in The New York Times, BBC, and Financial Times. She maintains an active social media presence with over 500,000 followers."
Depois: "In a 2024 New York Times interview, she argued that AI regulation should focus on outcomes rather than methods."

---

### 3. Análises superficiais com terminações em -ing

Palavras de alerta: highlighting/underscoring/emphasizing..., ensuring..., reflecting/symbolizing..., contributing to..., cultivating/fostering..., encompassing..., showcasing...

Problema: frases com gerúndio adicionadas para aparentar profundidade que não existe.

Antes: "The color palette resonates with the region's natural beauty, symbolizing bluebonnets, reflecting the community's deep connection to the land."
Depois: "The architect chose blue and green to reference local bluebonnets and the Gulf coast."

---

### 4. Linguagem promocional

Palavras de alerta: boasts a, vibrant, rich (figurativo), profound, enhancing its, showcasing, exemplifies, commitment to, nestled, in the heart of, groundbreaking, renowned, breathtaking, must-visit, stunning

Problema: tom de anúncio publicitário em vez de texto neutro.

Antes: "Nestled within the breathtaking region of Gonder, Alamata stands as a vibrant town with a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty."
Depois: "Alamata is a town in the Gonder region of Ethiopia, known for its weekly market and 18th-century church."

---

### 5. Atribuições vagas e weasel words

Palavras de alerta: Industry reports, Observers have cited, Experts argue, Some critics argue

Problema: opiniões atribuídas a autoridades vagas sem fontes específicas.

Antes: "Experts believe it plays a crucial role in the regional ecosystem."
Depois: "The river supports several endemic fish species, according to a 2019 survey by the Chinese Academy of Sciences."

---

### 6. Seções formulaicas de "Desafios e Perspectivas"

Palavras de alerta: Despite its... faces several challenges..., Despite these challenges, Challenges and Legacy, Future Outlook

Problema: seções de desafios genéricas que não acrescentam informação real.

Antes: "Despite its prosperity, the city faces challenges typical of urban areas. Despite these challenges, it continues to thrive."
Depois: "Traffic congestion increased after 2015 when three new IT parks opened."

---

## Padrões de linguagem e gramática

### 7. Vocabulário AI sobreutilizado

Palavras de alto risco: Additionally, align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight (verbo), interplay, intricate/intricacies, key (adjetivo), landscape (substantivo abstrato), pivotal, showcase, tapestry (abstrato), testament, underscore (verbo), valuable, vibrant

Problema: aparecem com frequência muito maior em textos pós-2023 e costumam aparecer juntas.

Antes: "Additionally, an enduring testament to Italian influence is the widespread adoption of pasta in the local culinary landscape, showcasing how these dishes have integrated into the traditional diet."
Depois: "Pasta dishes, introduced during Italian colonization, remain common, especially in the south."

---

### 8. Evitar "é"/"são" (copula avoidance)

Palavras de alerta: serves as/stands as/marks/represents [a], boasts/features/offers [a]

Problema: construções elaboradas substituem cópulas simples.

Antes: "Gallery 825 serves as LAAA's exhibition space. The gallery features four spaces and boasts over 3,000 square feet."
Depois: "Gallery 825 is LAAA's exhibition space. The gallery has four rooms totaling 3,000 square feet."

---

### 9. Paralelismos negativos

Problema: construções "Not only...but..." ou "It's not just about..., it's..." são sobreutilizadas.

Antes: "It's not just about the beat riding under the vocals; it's part of the aggression and atmosphere. It's not merely a song, it's a statement."
Depois: "The heavy beat adds to the aggressive tone."

---

### 10. Regra dos três excessiva

Problema: ideias forçadas em grupos de três para parecer completo.

Antes: "The event features keynote sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities. Attendees can expect innovation, inspiration, and industry insights."
Depois: "The event includes talks and panels, with time for informal networking between sessions."

---

### 11. Variação elegante (synonym cycling)

Problema: penalidade de repetição nos LLMs causa substituição excessiva de sinônimos.

Antes: "The protagonist faces many challenges. The main character must overcome obstacles. The central figure eventually triumphs. The hero returns home."
Depois: "The protagonist faces many challenges but eventually triumphs and returns home."

---

### 12. Falsos intervalos (false ranges)

Problema: construções "from X to Y" onde X e Y não estão em uma escala significativa.

Antes: "Our journey has taken us from the singularity of the Big Bang to the grand cosmic web, from the birth of stars to the enigmatic dance of dark matter."
Depois: "The book covers the Big Bang, star formation, and current theories about dark matter."

---

## Padrões de estilo

### 13. Excesso de em-dash

Problema: LLMs usam em-dashes mais que humanos, imitando escrita de vendas "incisiva".

Antes: "The term is promoted by Dutch institutions—not by the people themselves. You don't say 'Netherlands, Europe'—yet this mislabeling continues—even in official documents."
Depois: "The term is promoted by Dutch institutions, not by the people themselves. The mislabeling continues in official documents."

---

### 14. Excesso de negrito

Problema: ênfase em negrito aplicada mecanicamente.

Antes: "It blends **OKRs** (Objectives and Key Results), **KPIs** (Key Performance Indicators), and visual tools such as the **Business Model Canvas**."
Depois: "It blends OKRs, KPIs, and visual tools like the Business Model Canvas."

---

### 15. Listas com headers inline em negrito

Problema: listas onde cada item começa com header em negrito seguido de dois-pontos.

Antes:
- **User Experience:** The user experience has been significantly improved.
- **Performance:** Performance has been enhanced through optimized algorithms.
- **Security:** Security has been strengthened with encryption.

Depois: "The update improves the interface, speeds up load times, and adds end-to-end encryption."

---

### 16. Title Case em cabeçalhos

Problema: maiúsculas em todas as palavras principais dos títulos.

Antes: "Strategic Negotiations And Global Partnerships"
Depois: "Strategic negotiations and global partnerships"

---

### 17. Emojis em estrutura

Problema: decoração de títulos e bullets com emojis.

Antes: "🚀 Launch Phase: The product launches in Q3 💡 Key Insight: Users prefer simplicity ✅ Next Steps: Schedule follow-up"
Depois: "The product launches in Q3. User research showed preference for simplicity. Next step: schedule a follow-up."

---

### 18. Aspas curvas (curly quotes)

Problema: ChatGPT usa aspas tipográficas ("...") em vez de aspas retas ("...").

Substituir: `"` `"` → `"` `"`

---

## Padrões de comunicação

### 19. Artefatos de comunicação colaborativa

Palavras de alerta: I hope this helps, Of course!, Certainly!, You're absolutely right!, Would you like..., let me know, here is a...

Problema: texto de resposta de chatbot colado como conteúdo.

Antes: "Here is an overview of the French Revolution. I hope this helps! Let me know if you'd like me to expand."
Depois: "The French Revolution began in 1789 when financial crisis and food shortages led to widespread unrest."

---

### 20. Disclaimers de corte de conhecimento

Palavras de alerta: as of [date], Up to my last training update, While specific details are limited/scarce..., based on available information...

Antes: "While specific details about the company's founding are not extensively documented, it appears to have been established in the 1990s."
Depois: "The company was founded in 1994, according to its registration documents."

---

### 21. Tom servil/sycophantic

Problema: linguagem excessivamente positiva e condescendente.

Antes: "Great question! You're absolutely right that this is a complex topic. That's an excellent point about the economic factors."
Depois: "The economic factors you mentioned are relevant here."

---

## Filler e hedging

### 22. Frases de enchimento

| Antes | Depois |
|---|---|
| "In order to achieve this goal" | "To achieve this" |
| "Due to the fact that it was raining" | "Because it was raining" |
| "At this point in time" | "Now" |
| "In the event that you need help" | "If you need help" |
| "The system has the ability to process" | "The system can process" |
| "It is important to note that the data shows" | "The data shows" |

---

### 23. Hedging excessivo

Problema: qualificações em excesso que diluem qualquer afirmação.

Antes: "It could potentially possibly be argued that the policy might have some effect on outcomes."
Depois: "The policy may affect outcomes."

---

### 24. Conclusões genéricas positivas

Problema: encerramentos vagos e otimistas sem conteúdo real.

Antes: "The future looks bright for the company. Exciting times lie ahead as they continue their journey toward excellence."
Depois: "The company plans to open two more locations next year."

---

## Personalidade e alma

Evitar AI patterns é só metade do trabalho. Escrita estéril e sem voz é tão óbvia quanto slop.

Sinais de escrita sem alma (mesmo tecnicamente "limpa"):
- Todas as frases têm o mesmo comprimento e estrutura
- Só reporta fatos, sem reação
- Nenhuma incerteza ou sentimentos mistos
- Sem humor, sem personalidade
- Soa como Wikipedia ou press release

Como adicionar voz:
- Ter opiniões. Reagir aos fatos, não apenas listá-los.
- Variar o ritmo. Frases curtas. Depois umas mais longas que levam mais tempo para chegar onde vão.
- Reconhecer complexidade. "Isso é impressionante mas também meio perturbador" bate "Isso é impressionante."
- Usar "eu" quando cabe. Primeira pessoa não é pouco profissional — é honesta.
- Deixar um pouco de bagunça entrar. Estrutura perfeita parece algorítmica.
- Ser específico sobre sentimentos. Não "isso é preocupante" mas "tem algo inquietante em agentes trabalhando às 3h da manhã enquanto ninguém está olhando."
